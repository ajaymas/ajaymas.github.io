About me

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of North Carolina at Wilmington, from Fall 2024. Previously, I was a visiting assistant professor at Lenoir-Rhyne University and a postdoctoral researcher at Penn State, the University of Arizona, and Florida International University. I obtained my Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology, Karnataka, India.
